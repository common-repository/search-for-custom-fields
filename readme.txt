Create your own fields for your posts / pages and propose a search based on these fields to your visitors.

== Description ==

Boost the custom fields of Wordpress with this plugin: <br> <br>
- Simplified creation of fields when writing your posts and pages. <br>
- Activate the widget to propose to your visitors the search on your fields.
- Customize the result of the search results in the options of the plugin. <br>
- Insert the shortcode in your content to display the fields. <br>
- Insert a search form in your pages or articles. <br> <br>


== Installation ==

To install the plugin: <br> <br>
- Unpack the ZIP file, <br>
- Place the decompressed folder in the wp-content / plugins / folder of your site, <br>
- In the administration of your site, go to "Extensions" then click on "Activate" for the plugin "Search For Custom Fields", <br>
- A new "Search For Custom Fields" menu will appear. <br>
